About Chaitali

Bridal, editorial & celebrity makeup artist and makeup educator.

A makeup artist known for elaborate bridal design work and polished on screen looks, Chaitali has spent years building a practice around one idea: makeup should honour the face it's on, not compete with it.

Her work spans intricate bridal styling, traditional draping, jewellery forward finishes, and long wear techniques for multi day celebrations, through to clean, camera ready looks for editorial shoots and entertainment industry clients. Every appointment starts with a conversation about skin, occasion, and how you want to feel in the photographs that outlast the day itself.

Alongside bridal and event bookings, Chaitali teaches makeup classes for beginners and working artists who want to sharpen their technique, see Classes.
