# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Prospective bridal and event clients researching a makeup artist before booking, entertainment-industry contacts and stylists scouting talent, and aspiring makeup artists considering Chaitali's makeup classes. They arrive from Instagram (@facesbychaitalisengupta) or word of mouth, on mobile most of the time, and are evaluating portfolio quality, credibility, and how to get in touch or book.

## Product Purpose
A single-site portfolio and booking front door for Chaitali Sengupta, a makeup artist specializing in bridal, party, and editorial/celebrity makeup, who also teaches makeup classes. Success = a visitor trusts the work within seconds, browses the portfolio, understands services/pricing tiers and class offerings, and contacts/books via WhatsApp, phone, or Instagram.

## Brand Personality
Elegant and refined. Understated sophistication and soft luxury rather than loud glamour — confident, polished, warm but never fussy. The site should feel like a well-produced editorial spread, not a salon flyer.

## Anti-references
Avoid the generic "beauty salon" template look: no pink-and-gold gradients, no glitter/sparkle textures, no stock-photo hero banners, no cluttered service-card grids. Nothing that reads as a cheap WordPress beauty theme.

## Design Principles
- Let the portfolio photography carry the visual weight; UI chrome stays quiet.
- Elegance through restraint — generous whitespace, refined type, a controlled two-tone palette (red + beige) rather than decoration.
- One clear path to contact/booking from every section.
- Credibility over cuteness: real service detail, real class info, no filler copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard WCAG AA: verified color contrast for the red/beige palette, keyboard-navigable nav and forms, alt text on all portfolio images, respects prefers-reduced-motion.
